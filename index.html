My History

    Undergraduate @ TJU
    Year 2011 marks the commencement of a new stage in my life. With curiosity and excitement, I left my hometown and went to the Tongji University(TJU) . Campus life was far more diverse than I had anticipated. While many buddies were busy with dining, dating and gaming, I was obsessed with exotic hobbies like programming and wandering about tech forums.
  • 2015 - 2018
    Master @ TJU

Here, I began to publish papers, write blogs, and contribute to open source projects. I got very lucky in these years.
